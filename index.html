About Me

Hi! My name is Maria Waslick! I was born in New York City, but I currently live in Longmeadow, Massachusetts. I graduated from Vassar College in 2017 with a Psychology major, and have been working in various day cares and elementary schools for the past few years. However, I have always been interested in coding and technology, have prior experience with graphic design, and am excited to take the plunge into UW's Coding Boot Camp. I cannot wait to learn more about coding and what goes into creating websites and applications. When I'm not coding, I enjoy reading, writing, drawing, going for walks, and playing video games. I also have a dog named Ping, who is an 11-year-old Shih Tzu and likes to sit on the couch next to me.

I hope you enjoy my first portfolio site! You can visit my Github to see my other coding projects.
